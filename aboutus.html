Lovely Professional Univ.com is a high-end property portal that caters to a global market with its unique services and novel online features. Having been launched in the year 2020 by ABHI & Company, Lovely Professional Univ. has quickly risen to being the No. 1 Property Portal in India. The Lovely Professional Univ. design is based on rigorous research, unique product developments, and innovative initiative which has been readily accepted by users. In an attempt to best serve the users, features on the Lovely Professional Univ. realty portal are constantly invented, evaluated and upgraded. In addition to all else, Lovely Professional Univ.com is synonymous with internationally acclaimed property fairs. It is here that top-notch properties of major Indian metros are showcased for audiences in India and abroad. Till date, Lovely Professional Univ.com has successfully hosted 75 domestic and over 15 international property shows around the globe.
